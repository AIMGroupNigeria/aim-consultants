Designs

The design excellence of AIM’s project is reflected in the multitude of design awards and citations as well as in the international clientele that regularly seek the services of AIM’s design professionals.

 

The design of a structure or complex or infrastructure begins with programming and feasibility studies and ends with a completed project and a satisfied client. Between these two points , the professional expertise to insure that all client objectives are fully met in the areas of conceptual design, structural engineering , mechanical and electrical systems design and construction management is required. AIM’s integrated design and engineering teams have proven themselves capable to master the most challenging of projects and stand ready to continue to turn desires and ideas into realities that reflect climatic and cultural influences, energy efficiency, permanence, economy and beauty.

Design Aspects:

  • Analysis of briefs & approvals by client
  • Concept design & approvals by client
  • Preliminary design & coordination of engineering discipline
  • Detailed design & working drawings
